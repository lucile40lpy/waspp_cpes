DISCOVER WASPP


This research project explores the link between student profiles and their preferences for different pedagogical aspects. We are driven by curiosity to understand the specific dynamics within the CPPESMD context.

The questionnaire covers several dimensions of students' academic life. Questions deal with students' socio-demographic profile, academic behaviors, personal motivations and pedagogical preferences. WASPP stands for "What Are Students' Pedagogical Preferences?". To try and answer this question, we look into students' academic behaviours and motivations. The data used to study these subjects is collected through the WASPP questionnaire, which covers several dimensions of students' academic life. Questions deal with students' socio-demographic profile, academic behaviors, personal motivations and pedagogical preferences.

All answers are anonymous. The questions are varied to capture a holistic view, but nothing is mandatory: participation is entirely optional.
