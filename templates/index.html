TAKE THE WASPP TEST

This research project explores the link between student profiles and their preferences for different pedagogical aspects. We are driven by curiosity to understand the specific dynamics within the CPES & SMD context.

The questionnaire covers four main dimensions of your student life:

  • • Socio-demographic profile
  • • Academic behaviors
  • • Personal motivations
  • • Pedagogical preferences

Note: All answers are anonymous. The questions are varied to capture a holistic view, but nothing is mandatory: participation is entirely optional.
