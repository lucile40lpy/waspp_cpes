WASPP Questionnaire

A few things to know before answering the questionnaire:

  • It is anonymous: All answers are completely confidential.
  • All questions are optional: Nothing is mandatory; skip what you prefer not to answer.
  • It is varied: Questions cover a wide range of experiences as a student, including your academic habits, your personal motivations and your pedagogical preferences.
  • The Goal: We are just curious about exploring CPESSMD students' profiles!

Please answer with what you think is true, not what you think you should answer.
